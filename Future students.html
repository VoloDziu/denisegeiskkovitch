Future Graduate Students

Applying to graduate school can be overwhelming... 😓

Figuring out which programs and supervisors might be a good fit, writing a compelling statement of purpose, and trying to figure out how (and when) to email potential supervisors can be challenging without the right information and mentorship.


Whether you’re unsure where to start, second-guessing your Statement of Purpose, or stuck wondering what professors really want to hear from you, I’m here to help. ✅


I started this workshop because I kept receiving emails from prospective students who did not have access to good advice or mentorship to put together a promising application.


In this workshop, I will help you gain clarity and direction into how to apply to graduate school, what the most important aspects of your application are, and the most common mistakes students make.
