Research and Design

Hi, I'm Denise.

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Conenctions Lab at Simon Fraser University. I am interested in research and design, and have experience conducting, analyzing, and presenting human-computer interaction, human-robot interaction, and psychological research with adults and children.
I will be an Assistant Professor at McMaster University starting July 2022.

Why Research?

Research provides us with answers to unknown questions, about how people percepive and interact with teachnology. My goal is to expand our knowledge of how technology affects humans, and vice versa, and therefore create technologies that benefit us.
